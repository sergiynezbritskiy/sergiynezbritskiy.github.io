## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Sergiy Nezbritskiy ("I", "me", "my") handles personal data in connection with Magento 2 extensions sold on the [Adobe Commerce Marketplace](https://commercemarketplace.adobe.com/). It applies to interactions through this website and any support communication related to my extensions.

## Purchases and Licensing

All purchases, payments, and license management for my extensions are handled exclusively by Adobe Commerce Marketplace. I do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or Adobe account credentials. For information about how Adobe processes your purchase data, please refer to the [Adobe Privacy Policy](https://www.adobe.com/privacy/policy.html).

## Data I Collect

### Support and Technical Assistance

When you contact me for support, I may collect:

- **Email address** — to communicate with you and follow up on your issue.
- **Order or license information** — to verify your purchase and provide authorized support.
- **Technical details about your environment** — such as Magento version, PHP version, extension configuration, or error logs that you voluntarily share to help diagnose an issue.

I collect only the information you choose to provide. I do not remotely access your store or collect data from it automatically.

### Website Contact

If you reach out to me via email or any contact form on this site, I will store your email address and the content of your message in order to respond to your inquiry.

## How I Use Your Data

- **Support communication** — to respond to your questions and resolve technical issues with my extensions.
- **License verification** — to confirm you hold a valid license before providing support or sending extension updates.
- **Product improvement** — anonymized technical feedback (e.g., common issues reported) may inform future development, but no personally identifiable information is used for this purpose.

I do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent, and I do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

## Data Retention

I retain support correspondence and related technical information for as long as reasonably necessary to resolve your issue and provide follow-up assistance. If you would like your data deleted, please contact me and I will do so promptly.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request access to the personal data I hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
- Withdraw consent for me to hold your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at [sergiy.nezbritskiy@gmail.com](mailto:sergiy.nezbritskiy@gmail.com).

## Third-Party Services

- **GitHub Pages** — this website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect technical data such as IP addresses as part of their hosting infrastructure. See the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
